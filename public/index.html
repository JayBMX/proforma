Pro-Forma

Offline-first Form

Define your form fields

To begin collecting data with Pro-forma, you first need to define the fields you would like to collect.

  • Drag and drop text boxes, radio buttons and checkboxes from the palette on the right to the empty form on the left
  • Click on each form item to choose its name, value and characteristics
  • When you're ready, hit "Save" at the bottom to save your form and begin data collection
